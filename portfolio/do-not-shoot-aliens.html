DO NOT Shoot Aliens

Welcome to a unique gaming experience where precision and strategy take center stage. In "DO NOT Shoot Aliens," the objective is clear: avoid shooting aliens and reach as many checkpoints as possible. Navigate the challenges, collect Ethereums, and enhance your gameplay with a variety of skins and upcoming weapons in the in-game shop.

Key Features:

Avoid Shooting Aliens:
  • The primary challenge is to resist the temptation to shoot aliens. Shooting them triggers aggression, turning them red and prompting an attack.
  • Encourage players to strategize and find alternative ways to progress without resorting to violence.
Dynamic AI Behavior:
  • Aliens react dynamically to player actions, showcasing a range of emotions from curiosity to anger based on player behavior.
  • Create an engaging and responsive game world that reacts to player choices.
Consequences for Shooting:
  • The game emphasizes non-violent interactions, rewarding players for choosing a peaceful approach.
  • Shooting red aliens results in a decrease in your overall score.
Checkpoint Navigation:
  • Test your agility and reflexes as you navigate through various checkpoints spread across the game environment.
  • Each checkpoint reached contributes to your score, challenging players to achieve their personal best.
Ethereum Collection:
  • Collect Ethereums scattered throughout the game environment to unlock new skins.
  • The in-game shop allows players to customize their character, promoting a sense of personalization and achievement.
Shop for Skins and Weapons:
  • Accumulate Ethereums to access the in-game shop, offering a variety of skins for your character.
  • Future updates will introduce a range of weapons for purchase, adding an extra layer of strategy to the gameplay.
